      I'm a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Stockholm Centre for the Ethics of War and Peace, which is based at the Department of Philosophy, Stockholm University. As part of this, I'm also the current editor of the Public Ethics blog.

      My research focuses primarily on the ethics of self-defence and punishment, as well as the ethics of advising, demanding, and blaming.

      I received my PhD in Philosophy from King's College London in July 2023, supervised by David Owens, Massimo Renzo, and Sarah Fine. Before that, I obtained a BPhil in Philosophy from the University of Oxford, and a BA in Philosophy from King's College London.

      I occasionally also make digital art.
