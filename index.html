      Jonas Haeg

      Postdoctoral Researcher, Stockholm Centre for the Ethics of War and Peace
      Department of Philosophy, Stockholm University

      I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Stockholm Centre for the Ethics of War and Peace, which is based at the Department of Philosophy at Stockholm University. I am also the current editor of the Public Ethics blog.

      My research focuses primarily on the ethics of self-defence and punishment, as well as the ethics of advising, demanding, and blaming.

      I received my PhD in Philosophy from King's College London in July 2023, supervised by David Owens, Massimo Renzo, and Sarah Fine. Before that, I obtained an MPhil in Philosophy from the University of Oxford, and a BA in Philosophy from King's College London.
